At Root Studio we build personalities through branding, bring ideas into reality through our love for print and develop engaging experiences through web design and digital marketing expertise.

  • Digital Marketing
    We produce the best digital experiences whether it's content marketing, e-commerce design, web applications or e-learning platforms. We make 'digital' work hard for your business.
  • Branding
    We create memorable brands whether we're working together to develop an established business or guiding you through the exciting process of launching a brand new product
  • Design for print
    Working with trusted printers across the UK, we produce great quality printed products including anything from flyers, brochures and prospectuses to banners, signage and more.
